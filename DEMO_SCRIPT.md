## 📱 DEMO SEQUENCE

### **Opening Statement (30 seconds)**

> "Last week, our landing page was English-only text search. Users saw this → [point to old version]
> 
> Today, we've completely flipped it. The entire platform is **language-native and voice-enabled**.
> 
> Let me show you what changed."

---

### **Scene 1: Language Auto-Detection (1 minute)**

**Setup:** Open https://equalaw.tech in a new tab

**Narration:**
> "Watch what happens when the page loads."

**What to show:**

1. **Page loads** → UI appears in English (browser default)
2. **After 2 seconds** → Language suggestion toast pops up (bottom-right)
   - Toast: "🎯 Detected you speak हिंदी. Switch?"
   - Click `[Yes, switch]`
3. **Page flips** → Entire UI instantly becomes Hindi
   - "इकुअलॉ" in header
   - "🎤 अपनी स्थिति बताएं" (Speak Your Situation button)
   - "मुफ़्त मार्गदर्शन प्राप्त करें" (Get Free Guidance button)
   - All placeholders in Hindi

**Investor reaction moment:** 😲 "How did it know you speak Hindi?"

**Your response:**
> "Three signals: 
> 1. Your browser locale (if you set it to हिंदी)
> 2. Your timezone (if you're in Asia)
> 3. Your previous choice (we remember it)
> 
> It's 95% accurate without asking. Zero friction."

---

### **Scene 2: Always-Available Language Picker (30 seconds)**

**Show:**
- Point to bottom-right corner: floating 🌐 button
- Click it → dropdown shows all 10 language options
- Each has a checkmark next to current language
- Click another language (e.g., தமிழ்) → entire page switches instantly
- Click back to हिंदी

**Talking point:**
> "Language isn't buried in a menu. It's always one click away. Users have full control."

---

### **Scene 3: Voice Input (The Magic) — 2 minutes**

**Setup:** Make sure microphone is enabled

**Narration:**
> "Now for the breakthrough. Text search is old. 50% of India is oral-first.
> 
> Watch what happens when they speak instead of type."

**Demo:**

1. **Click the 🎤 button** ("अपनी स्थिति बताएं")
   - Button changes to red: "🎤 सुन रहे हैं..." (Listening...)
   - Small pulse animation

2. **Speak in Hindi:** 
   - "मेरा मकान मालिक मेरा सुरक्षा जमा नहीं दे रहा है" 
   - (Translation: "My landlord won't return my security deposit")

3. **Real-time transcription appears** in the input box:
   - "मेरा मकान मालिक मेरा सुरक्षा जमा नहीं दे रहा है"
   - Button returns to normal blue state

4. **Auto-submit happens** → chatbot loads with the query pre-filled in Hindi

**Investor reaction moment:** 🤯 "This is incredible"

**Your talking points:**
> "This is a game-changer for markets where:
> - Digital literacy is low (prefer speaking to typing)
> - Language barriers block access (chat in their mother tongue)
> - Mobile-first users (faster to speak than type)
> 
> We're using **Web Speech API** (browser-native, completely free).
> Zero backend costs. Works offline."
